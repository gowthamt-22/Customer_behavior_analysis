🚀 Excited to share my latest data analytics project — Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis!

I analyzed a dataset of 3,900+ retail customer transactions across 18 attributes including age, product category, purchase amount, season, payment method, and review ratings to extract meaningful business insights.

📊 Here's what I did:
• Performed EDA and SQL-based querying to segment customers by purchase frequency, discount usage, and spending patterns
• Built an interactive Power BI dashboard visualizing category-wise revenue, seasonal trends, and shipping preferences
• Identified weekly-frequency subscribers as the highest-spend customer profile — a key insight for retention and upsell strategy

🛠️ Tools Used: Python (Pandas) | SQL | Power BI


This project strengthened my ability to turn raw transactional data into actionable business decisions — something I'm passionate about as I grow in the data analytics space.
